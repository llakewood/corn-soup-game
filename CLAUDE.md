# Corn Soup — A Story of Community

Phaser 3 storytelling game based on the "Stories From The Land" documentary about Haudenosaunee corn soup traditions.

## Structure

- `index.html` — Entry point, loads Google Fonts (Cormorant Garamond + Lora) and Phaser 3
- `game.js` — Full game logic (~1700 lines), all textures procedurally generated

## Gameplay

- **Level 1**: Harvest corn by dragging ears from stalks
- **Level 2**: Husk corn by tapping/dragging husks off
- **Level 3**: Cooking — build a fire from logs, then add ingredients (corn, beans, squash, salt pork)
- Narrative interludes between levels with cinematic letterboxing
- Salt pork origin story explains how pork entered the recipe through colonization

## Design

- Warm earth-tone palette, cinematic documentary aesthetic
- Ambient particle effects, staggered text reveals, progress dots
- Procedural textures (no external image assets)

## Running

Open `index.html` in a browser or serve locally.
